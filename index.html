Dal Baati Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (for dough) + extra for serving
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • 1 cup mixed dal (toor, moong, chana)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped)

Instructions

  1. For Baati: Mix wheat flour, semolina, salt, and ghee. Add water and knead a stiff dough. Shape into round balls. Bake them in a preheated oven at 180°C for 30-35 minutes (flip once) or roast over coal/tandoor until golden.
  2. For Dal: Pressure cook mixed dal with turmeric, salt, and enough water for 3-4 whistles. Whisk and keep aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard and cumin seeds. Add onions, sauté till translucent. Add tomatoes, chilies, and spices, cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the boiled dal, simmer for 10 minutes. Finish with coriander leaves and garam masala.
  5. To Serve: Break hot baatis, pour hot dal over them, drizzle lots of ghee! Enjoy with a pickle and fresh salad.
स्वादिष्ट दाल बाटी का आनंद लें! (Enjoy your delicious Dal Baati!)
